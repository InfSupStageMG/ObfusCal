# 9. Architectural Decisions (ADR Log)

In this project, we use **Architecture Decision Records (ADR)** to capture decisions made related
to system design and engineering practices.

We do this to record decisions taken, including the information to understand the context that
led to a given decision as well as its consequences, preventing us from having the same discussions over and over again.

Please select a decision from the navigation menu to view the context, considered options,
and rationale.
